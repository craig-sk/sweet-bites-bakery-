About Sweet Bites Bakery

Our Cape Town Story

Sweet Bites Bakery opened its doors in Cape Town in 2018. What started as a tiny home kitchen in the heart of the Mother City quickly grew into a beloved family-run business serving the local community.

From our humble beginnings baking for neighbors in our Cape Town home, we've expanded to become a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike, bringing the warmth of homemade baked goods to the bustling streets of Cape Town.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To unite people by providing reasonably priced, superior baked goods to the Cape Town community.

Vision: To continue expanding across Cape Town and improve our bakery's online accessibility for our local customers.

Our Cape Town Community

We proudly serve Cape Town event planners, working adults, students, and local families who have supported us since day one. Our bakery has become a gathering place for the Cape Town community, where people come together over fresh baked goods.
